This project was worked on for my Senior project throughout the Summer and Fall of 2020.
I mainly contributed to the group feature and the StudentsSection page of the website.
The group feature is for the purpose of dividing the students in groups for each of
their classes. These groups would then have assignments that can help each other boost
the number of points throughout the course. The StudentsSection page demonstrates the
list of students in the course and the groups that they are a part of. The admin can
see all of the groups of a course whereas the students can see their own group in a course.

but the features that have been implemented thus far include the UI, weapon swapping, enemy behavior,
and a collision system between the enemies and the player. This was worked on through the use of C#
and the Unity Engine.
